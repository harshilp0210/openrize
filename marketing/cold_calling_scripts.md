# 📞 The "Master" Cold Call Script (High Conversion)

This is the **Gold Standard** script. It works because it is respectful, honest, and focuses on **curiosity**, not selling.

**Context:** You are calling to book a 10-minute demo, NOT to sell a $200/month plan.

---

### The Call Flow

**1. The Opener (Permission-Based)**
**(Ring Ring)**
**Gatekeeper/Owner:** "Elk Grove Dental, this is Sarah."
**YOU:** "Hi Sarah, my name is [Your Name]. I’m a local developer here in Elk Grove. I know you weren't expecting my call, do you have 30 seconds to tell me if I should hang up?"
*(Wait for the chuckle or "Sure, go ahead".)*

**2. The Problem (The Hook)**
**YOU:** "Thanks. I was actually just on your website to verify your address, and I noticed something specific. You guys have a great 4.9-star rating, but honestly, your **'Book Appointment'** process on mobile is a little harder than it needs to be."

**3. The Insight (Why it matters)**
**YOU:** "I help local clinics fix this so they don't lose patients to competitors like [Competitor Name] who have easy online booking."

**4. The Ask (Low Friction)**
**YOU:** "I’m not trying to sell you anything right now. But would you be open to me sending a 2-minute video showing exactly where the issue is and how to fix it?"

---

### Scenario A: They say "Sure, send it."
**YOU:** "Perfect. What’s the best email for you?"
*(Get Email)*
**YOU:** "Awesome. I'll send that over in 10 minutes. If you like what you see, we can chat later. Thanks Sarah!"
*(Hang up -> Record Video -> Send Strategy 1 Email)*

### Scenario B: They say "We're fine / Not interested."
**YOU:** "Totally fair. Just out of curiosity, are you happy with your current volume of new patients from Google, or is it just a bad time to chat?"
*(This often opens the conversation back up).*

---

## 🏎️ Speed Scripts (Short & Sweet)

### Script 1: The "I Found a Problem" (Best for Gatekeepers)

*Use this when a receptionist answers.*

**YOU:** "Hi, I promise I'm not a robot. My name is [Your Name], I'm a local developer here in Elk Grove. I was just on your website and I saw something that's actually blocking people from booking appointments properly. Could I speak to the owner or office manager for 30 seconds to tell them what it is?"

**(If they ask what it is):**
"Basically, on iPhone, your 'Book Now' button is hidden. I just wanted to let them know so they don't lose patients today."

---

## Script 2: The "Pattern Interrupt" (Best for Owners)

*Use this if you get the owner directly.*

**YOU:** "Hey [Name], this is a cold call, do you want to hang up on me now or give me 30 seconds?"
*(They will laugh or say go ahead)*

**YOU:** "Thanks. I'm right down the street. I specialize in helping Dentists get more Google Reviews. I noticed you have 4.9 stars which is awesome, but your competitor [Name] is beating you because they have 50 more reviews."

**YOU:** "I have a system that texts your patients automatically so you get 5-10 new reviews a week without asking. Would you be open to a 10-minute demo next Tuesday?"

---

## Script 3: The "Free Audit" (Soft Sell)

**YOU:** "Hi, my name is [Your Name] from Openrize. We're doing a project on the top dentists in Elk Grove and I saw your practice came up."

**YOU:** "I specialize in fixing website speed for medical clinics. I ran a quick test and saw your site is loading a bit slow on mobile data. I made a free video showing exactly how to fix it."

**YOU:** "What's the best email to send that to?"

---

## 🚫 Common Objections & Rebuttals

**"We already have a marketing guy."**
*   **YOU:** "That's great. Honestly, I'm not trying to replace them. I just build specific tools to automate reviews. Most marketing guys don't do the software side. I'm happy to work *with* them."

**"We're not interested."**
*   **YOU:** "Totally fair. Just so I don't bother you again—is it that you don't need more reviews, or is now just a bad time?"

**"How much is it?"**
*   **YOU:** "It depends on what you need, but most offices pay around $199 a month. But honestly, I'd rather show you it works first. I can set you up with a free trial next week?"
