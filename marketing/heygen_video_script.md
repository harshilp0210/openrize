# 🎬 Openrize Marketing Video Script (for HeyGen AI)

**Target Audience:** Local Business Owners (Restaurants, Services, etc.)
**Tone:** Professional, energetic, and results-oriented.
**Visual Style:** Clean background or office setting.

---

## The Script

**(0:00 - 0:15) The Hook**
**[Visual: Avatar smiling, confident]**
"Are you a local business owner tired of seeing customers go to your competitors? You know your service is better, but their online presence is just... winning. That’s where we come in. Welcome to Openrize."

**(0:15 - 0:35) The Website Solution**
**[Visual: Screen shows a sleek, mobile-friendly website mockup]**
"First, let's talk about your digital storefront. Most websites are just digital brochures that look terrible on mobile. At Openrize, we build high-performance websites designed with one goal: conversion. We make sure that when a customer finds you, they can book a table or request a quote in seconds—not minutes."

**(0:35 - 0:55) The Email & Review Engine**
**[Visual: Graphics of 5-star reviews popping up and email icons flying]**
"But a great website is useless if no one remembers you. That’s why we integrate powerful email and SMS automation. We help you reactivate your existing customer base and automatically generate unmatched social proof. Imagine getting consistent 5-star reviews on autopilot while bringing your past customers back through the door."

**(0:55 - 1:15) About Openrize**
**[Visual: Avatar with a sincere expression, hand on heart or open gesture]**
"Who is Openrize? We aren't just another faceless agency. We are your local growth partners. We specialize in helping businesses in [Your City/Area] dominate their market. We don't just sell you a service; we build you a system that brings you more revenue, week after week."

**(1:15 - 1:30) Call to Action**
**[Visual: Text overlay with website URL and 'Free Audit' button]**
"Stop leaving money on the table. improved rankings, more reviews, and a website that actually sells. Visit us at Openrize.com today, and let's turn your online presence into your best employee. See you there!"

---

## 🎨 Creative Direction for HeyGen
*   **Avatar:** Choose a friendly, professional avatar (e.g., 'Joshua' or 'Anna' in business casual).
*   **Pacing:** Set speed to 1.05x or 1.1x to keep it energetic.
*   **Gestures:** Use 'pointing' gestures during the Website and Email sections, and 'open hands' during the About section.
